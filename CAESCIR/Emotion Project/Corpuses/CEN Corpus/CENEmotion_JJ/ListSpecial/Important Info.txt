Important:
1. The emotion span contain emotion(s) and negation. Extract emotion and then apply the negation. 
Ex:  never know youth and gladness 

2. Has a combination of 2 emotions. Add all emotions for both of them
Ex: perfect love 
- Emotions in perfect & love
Ex: grow in confidence 
- Emotions in grow & confidence 

3: Opposite emotions are deleted 
Ex: grow weary 
- Grow joy 
- Weary sadness
